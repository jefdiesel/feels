Feels SMS Terms & Conditions

Opt-In

By entering your phone number in the Feels mobile app and tapping "Send Code", you consent to receive one-time verification codes via SMS from Feels. You must confirm you are 18 years or older to create an account.

Message Frequency

You will receive SMS messages only when you request a verification code. Message frequency varies based on your login activity.

STOP Instructions

To stop receiving SMS messages, reply STOP to any message. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent.

HELP Instructions

For help, reply HELP to any message, or contact us at support@feelsfun.app.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Check with your mobile carrier for details.

Your mobile information will not be sold or shared with third parties for promotional or marketing purposes. View our full Privacy Policy.
