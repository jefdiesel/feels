Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Information We Collect

When you use Feels, we collect:

  • Account Information: Phone number, email (optional), name, date of birth, and profile details you provide
  • Photos: Images you upload to your profile
  • Location: Your ZIP code to show you nearby matches
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, including likes, matches, and messages
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To show you potential matches based on your preferences
  • To facilitate messaging between matched users
  • To send you notifications about matches and messages
  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To ensure safety and prevent fraud

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only:

  • With other users as part of the matching and messaging features
  • With service providers who help us operate the app (hosting, SMS verification)
  • When required by law or to protect safety

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit and at rest. Messages are stored securely and photos are hosted on encrypted cloud storage.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Access and download your data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of promotional communications

Contact Us

For privacy questions or requests, contact us at privacy@feelsfun.app
