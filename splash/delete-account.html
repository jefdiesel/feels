Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. Here's how to permanently delete your Feels account and all associated data.

Option 1: Delete from inside the app (fastest)

  1. Open the Feels app and log in.
  2. Tap the Profile tab at the bottom.
  3. Tap the settings icon (top right).
  4. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.
  5. Type DELETE to confirm.
  6. Tap Delete My Account.

Your account is removed immediately.

Option 2: Request deletion by email

If you can't access the app, email us from the address associated with your account:

Email us to delete

We'll process your request within 7 days and send a confirmation when complete.

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

  • Your profile (name, age, bio, photos, prompts)
  • All matches and conversations
  • Messages you sent and received
  • Likes, passes, and feed history
  • Location data and search preferences
  • Push notification tokens
  • Any device IDs associated with your account

What may be retained

For legal and safety reasons, we may retain a limited record of:

  • Reports filed against you (if any) — for safety enforcement
  • Anonymized purchase records — for tax and accounting compliance
  • Server logs — automatically purged after 90 days

None of this retained data is personally identifiable after deletion.

Active subscriptions

If you have an active feels+ subscription, deleting your account does not cancel the subscription with Google Play. To cancel:

  1. Open Google Play Store on your device.
  2. Tap your profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions.
  3. Tap Feels and then Cancel subscription.

Cancel before deleting your account, otherwise you'll continue to be billed.
